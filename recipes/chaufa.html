Chaufa Recipe

Description

The Arroz Chaufa is an exquisite dish rooted on the Chinese cuisine, specifically in their fried rice, but with an special touch of the Peruvian sazon. Each household gives it its own style, making this a unique, must-try recipe!

Now pay close attention to the following ingredients and steps to impress your loved ones with a unforgettable Chaufa.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of Chinese onion
  • 1 spoon of ground garlic
  • 1 spoon of grated ginger
  • 1/2 cup of red bell pepper, diced into small cubes
  • 300g of meat
  • 4 spoons of sesame oil
  • 8 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of sugar
  • 1 pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat.
  2. Add half a cup of finely chopped Chinese onion, one tablespoon of ground garlic, and one tablespoon of grated ginger.
  3. Then add half a cup of diced red bell pepper and one cup of meat of your choice, browning everything for 2 minutes.
  4. Next, add 4 cups of rice, increase the heat to high, and stir for a few minutes before letting it rest.
  5. Then, add 8 cups of water and cook until the rice is done.
  6. Then add 4 tablespoons of sesame oil, 8 tablespoons of soy sauce, salt, pepper, a pinch of sugar, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, and 2 cups of green onion, stirring well.
  7. Add a 4-egg omelet, cut into strips, and stir to combine.
  8. Finally, you can add a few drops of lime and some homemade blended rocoto to taste.
