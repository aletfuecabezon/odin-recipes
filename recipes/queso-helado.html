Queso Helado

Description

A modern and delicious version of the traditional, Arequipean dessert: Queso Helado; with a unique twist that makes it irresistible. A simple recipe to enjoy at any time.

This exquisite 21st-century queso helado is made with fresh milk, shredded coconut, cinnamon, and cloves, achieving a unique and delicious flavor. Ideal to share on a special afternoon with your loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of fresh milk
  • ¾ cup of shredded coconut
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 8 cloves
  • 3 egg yolks
  • ¾ cup of sugar
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • Ground cinnamon
  • 12 ladyfinger biscuits

Steps

  1. To prepare this delicious dessert, heat the fresh milk in a large pot along with the coconut, cinnamon stick, and cloves over low heat, allowing the flavors to slowly infuse.
  2. Stir occasionally to ensure the mixture cooks evenly.
  3. Then, remove the pot from the heat and quickly add the beaten egg yolks, mixing carefully to combine all ingredients without cooking the yolks.
  4. Add the sugar and heat the mixture again, stirring constantly until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  5. Once the mixture is ready, add the evaporated milk and mix well before removing the pot from the heat to let it cool.
  6. Next, strain the mixture directly into a mold and let it rest in the freezer for at least one day.
  7. Before serving, let the dessert sit at room temperature for a few minutes to make it easier to cut into 4 x 4 cm squares.
  8. When serving, you can sprinkle a little ground cinnamon on top and accompany each portion with ladyfinger biscuits.
